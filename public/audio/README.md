# Gift Site — Audio Placeholders

Place your audio files here.

| Filename              | Used in           | Notes                                |
|-----------------------|-------------------|--------------------------------------|
| voice-note.mp3        | Voice Note section | Your recorded voice message (MP3 or M4A renamed to .mp3) |
| background-music.mp3  | Music player      | Soft background track — instrumental works best |

Tips:
- Record your voice note on your phone's Voice Memos app, then AirDrop/export as M4A or MP3.
- For background music: look for royalty-free ambient/piano music on Pixabay, Free Music Archive, or Artlist.
- Keep files < 5MB if possible for fast loading.
